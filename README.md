# Southville 8B National High School Portal (Edge)

The **Southville 8B NHS Edge** is a **comprehensive digital school portal system** developed for *Southville 8B National High School*, located in Rodriguez, Rizal, Philippines. This platform is designed to streamline and modernize academic and administrative operations by serving as a **centralized hub** for students, teachers, administrators, and parents.

The system is deployed across **web, mobile, and desktop platforms**, ensuring accessibility, inclusivity, and efficiency for all stakeholders.

## Key Features

* **Learning Management System (LMS)**

  * Digital lessons, quizzes, and assignments
  * Automated grading and progress tracking
  * Gamified learning with leaderboards and achievements
  * Exam integrity features (session/device validation, randomization, anti-cheating measures)
  * Certificates and performance analytics

* **Student Information Management**

  * Enrollment, profiles, and academic records
  * Gradebook and transcript generation

* **Scheduling & Calendar**

  * Class schedules and academic calendar
  * Event and activity management

* **Communication & Messaging**

  * Announcements and notifications
  * Internal messaging system for teachers, students, and parents

* **Finance & Administration**

  * Tuition and fee tracking
  * Payment records and reports

* **Cross-Platform Accessibility**

  * **Web**: Next.js + TailwindCSS
  * **Mobile**: Flutter
  * **Desktop**: .NET MAUI

## Objective

The project aims to **empower digital transformation** in the school by reducing manual processes, enhancing collaboration, and providing a seamless, secure, and scalable digital ecosystem for academic excellence.
