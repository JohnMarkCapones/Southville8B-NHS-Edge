Preamble
WHEREAS, the Southville 8B NHS Edge LMS (“System”) collects, stores, and processes personal data of students, teachers, parents/guardians, and staff for academic and administrative purposes;
WHEREAS, Southville 8B NHS recognizes its legal and ethical duty to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction;
WHEREAS, this Policy establishes rights, responsibilities, and procedures regarding personal data in alignment with RA 10173, GDPR principles, and international information security management standards;
NOW, THEREFORE, this Privacy Policy is hereby adopted and enforced.
________________________________________
1. Definitions
For purposes of this Policy:
  •	“Personal Data” refers to any information that identifies or can reasonably identify an individual, including but not limited to name, birthday, age, address, contact information, grades, IP address, and parent/guardian details.
  •	“Processing” includes any operation performed on personal data, such as collection, recording, storage, use, disclosure, or deletion.
  •	“Minor” means any student under 18 years of age.
  •	“Data Subject” means any individual whose personal data is collected and processed by the LMS.
  •	“Third Party” means any external service provider engaged in LMS operations, including but not limited to Zoho Mail, Supabase, Cloudflare, Railway, Redis, Sentry, Vercel Analytics, Datadog, and DigitalOcean Analytics.
________________________________________
2. Information Collected
The LMS may collect the following categories of data:
  •	Student Data: Full name, birthday, age, gender, address, parent/guardian information, grades, academic performance, club participation.
  •	Teacher/Staff Data: Name, contact information, teaching records, professional data.
  •	Technical Data: IP address, device/browser information, system usage logs.
  •	Parent/Guardian Data: Contact details for communication and verification.
________________________________________
3. Purpose of Collection and Use
Personal data shall be processed solely for academic, operational, and administrative purposes, including but not limited to:
  •	Enrollment, grading, and academic evaluation.
  •	Management of modules, quizzes, assignments, and school announcements.
  •	Communication of official events, clubs, and activities.
  •	Notification and messaging systems.
  •	Security monitoring, error detection, and system improvement using analytics and monitoring tools.
The LMS does not use personal data for commercial advertising.
________________________________________
4. Data Sharing and Cross-Border Transfers
The LMS does not sell personal data. However, certain data may be disclosed to third-party service providers strictly for system operation, including:
  •	Email Services: Zoho Mail for transactional communications.
  •	Infrastructure & Hosting: Supabase (database, authentication, storage, real-time services), Cloudflare (Zero Trust, R2 storage, security services), Redis (session caching), Railway (hosting).
  •	Monitoring & Analytics: Sentry, Vercel Analytics, Datadog, DigitalOcean Analytics.
Data may be transmitted and stored outside the Philippines due to reliance on cloud-based providers. All transfers are conducted with reasonable safeguards consistent with RA 10173, GDPR cross-border requirements, and ISO/IEC 27018 for data in cloud environments.
________________________________________
5. Data Storage and Security
The LMS adopts a layered security framework in alignment with ISO/IEC 27001 (ISMS), ISO/IEC 27017 (Cloud Security), ISO/IEC 27032 (Cybersecurity), and ISO/IEC 27035 (Incident Response), including:
  •	Supabase storage, authentication, and database encryption.
  •	Cloudflare Zero Trust, WAF, and SSL/TLS encryption.
  •	Redis access controls and secured session management.
  •	Railway hosting with controlled API security.
  •	Regular backups with secure deletion protocols (NIST SP 800-88, DoD 5220.22-M).
  •	Custom API-layer enforcement and audit logging.
________________________________________
6. Cookies and Tracking
The LMS uses cookies and similar technologies to:
  •	Maintain session integrity and authentication.
  •	Monitor platform performance.
  •	Collect analytics (via Sentry, Vercel Analytics, Datadog, DigitalOcean).
Users may adjust browser settings to disable cookies, though this may impact LMS functionality.
________________________________________
7. User Rights
Consistent with RA 10173 and GDPR principles, all Data Subjects have the right to:
  1.	Access their personal information.
  2.	Correct inaccurate or outdated records.
  3.	Request deletion of non-essential data (subject to retention requirements).
  4.	Data portability upon formal request.
  5.	Be informed of system policies and updates.
  6.	Object or restrict processing under certain conditions.
  7.	Receive breach notifications within 72 hours of confirmed incidents.
________________________________________
8. Children’s Privacy
As most students are minors, the LMS enforces additional protections:
  •	Parent/guardian consent is required for processing student information.
  •	Sensitive student data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  •	No profiling, commercial advertising, or unauthorized disclosure of minors’ information.
________________________________________
9. Data Retention and Deletion
The LMS follows its official Data Retention & Deletion Policy, summarized as:
  •	Courses & Lessons: Retained 5 years; archived after 3 years of inactivity.
  •	Assignments: Retained 2 years; archived after 1 year; purged after 2 years.
  •	Grades & Progress: Retained indefinitely.
  •	Audit & System Logs: Retained 2 years; securely rotated.
  •	Backups: Weekly and quarterly, retained 90 days; securely deleted thereafter.
  Data disposal follows NIST SP 800-88 and DoD 5220.22-M secure deletion standards.
________________________________________
10. Breach Notification and Incident Response
In the event of a data breach:
  •	The LMS will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery, consistent with GDPR standards.
  •	Detection and response are enabled through Cloudflare Security, Sentry, Datadog, DigitalOcean Security, audit logs, and custom monitoring tools.
  •	Incidents are managed in accordance with ISO/IEC 27035 (Incident Response) and ISO/IEC 22301 (Business Continuity).
