## Introduction

Welcome to the Southville 8B NHS Edge Student Portal! This comprehensive guide will help you navigate and make the most of your digital learning environment. The portal is your central hub for accessing course materials, submitting assignments, taking quizzes, communicating with teachers, and staying connected with school activities.

### What You'll Learn

By the end of this guide, you will be able to:
- Navigate the student dashboard confidently
- Access and download course materials
- Submit assignments and take quizzes online
- Track your grades and academic progress
- Participate in clubs and school events
- Use productivity tools to manage your studies
- Communicate effectively with teachers and classmates
- Troubleshoot common issues independently

### System Requirements

To use the Southville 8B NHS Edge portal, you need:
- **Internet Connection**: Stable broadband or mobile data
- **Web Browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
- **Device**: Desktop computer, laptop, tablet, or smartphone
- **Screen Resolution**: Minimum 1024x768 (larger recommended)

📝 **Note**: The portal works on both desktop and mobile devices, with a responsive design that adapts to your screen size.

## Getting Started

### First-Time Login

Your school account has been created by the school administration. Follow these steps to log in for the first time:

1. **Open your web browser** and navigate to the school portal URL:
   ```
   https://southville8b-nhs-edge.edu.ph
   ```

2. **Click the "Login" button** in the top-right corner of the homepage.

   ![Login Button](../images/homepage-login-button.png)

3. **Enter your credentials**:
   - **Email/Username**: Your school email (usually `lastname.firstname@southville8b.edu.ph`)
   - **Password**: Your temporary password (provided by the school)

   ![Login Form](../images/student-login-form.png)

4. **Click "Sign In"** to access your account.

5. **Change your password** (first-time login only):
   - You will be prompted to create a new, secure password
   - Use a combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
   - Password must be at least 8 characters long

   ![Change Password](../images/first-login-password-change.png)

6. **Verify your email** (if required):
   - Check your school email for a verification link
   - Click the link to verify your account

✅ **Success**: You're now logged into your student portal!

### Understanding the Interface

After logging in, you'll see the main student dashboard. Here's what each section does:

- **Top Navigation Bar**: Quick access to key features (Dashboard, Courses, Assignments, Grades)
- **Sidebar Menu**: Complete list of all portal features and tools
- **Main Content Area**: Displays the current page content
- **Profile Menu**: Access settings, profile, and logout (top-right corner)
- **Notifications**: View important alerts and messages (bell icon)

![Student Dashboard Overview](../images/student-dashboard-overview.png)

💡 **Tip**: Bookmark the portal URL for quick access. You can also add it to your home screen on mobile devices.

## Managing Your Profile

Your profile contains your personal information and preferences.

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Click your **profile picture or name** in the top-right corner
2. Select **"My Profile"** from the dropdown menu

### Viewing Profile Information

Your profile displays:
- **Basic Information**: Name, student ID, grade level, section
- **Contact Information**: Email, phone number
- **Academic Information**: Enrolled courses, GWA, adviser
- **Emergency Contacts**: Guardian information (view-only)

![Student Profile](../images/student-profile-view.png)

### Editing Your Profile

You can update certain information:

1. Click the **"Edit Profile"** button
2. Update the following fields:
   - Profile picture
   - Phone number
   - Bio/About me
   - Interests and hobbies
3. Click **"Save Changes"**

⚠️ **Warning**: You cannot change your name, student ID, email, or grade level. Contact the registrar if these need updating.

### Changing Your Profile Picture

1. In edit mode, click your current profile picture
2. Click **"Choose File"** to select an image from your device
3. Crop and adjust the image as desired
4. Click **"Upload"** to save

**Image Requirements**:
- Format: JPG, PNG, or GIF
- Maximum size: 5MB
- Recommended dimensions: 500x500 pixels
- Appropriate content (school guidelines apply)

### Updating Your Password

For security, change your password regularly:

1. Go to **Profile → Settings → Security**
2. Click **"Change Password"**
3. Enter your **current password**
4. Enter your **new password** (twice)
5. Click **"Update Password"**

![Change Password](../images/profile-change-password.png)

💡 **Tip**: Use a unique password that you don't use for other accounts. Consider using a password manager.

### Notification Preferences

Control what notifications you receive:

1. Go to **Profile → Settings → Notifications**
2. Toggle notifications on/off for:
   - New assignments
   - Quiz reminders
   - Grade updates
   - Announcements
   - Event reminders
   - Chat messages
3. Choose notification delivery method:
   - In-portal alerts
   - Email notifications
   - Both
4. Click **"Save Preferences"**

![Notification Settings](../images/notification-preferences.png)

### Privacy Settings

Manage who can see your information:

1. Go to **Profile → Settings → Privacy**
2. Configure:
   - **Profile Visibility**: Public, students only, or private
   - **Contact Information**: Visible or hidden from other students
   - **Club Memberships**: Show or hide on profile
3. Click **"Save Settings"**

📝 **Note**: Teachers and administrators can always view your complete profile regardless of privacy settings.

### Browsing Learning Modules

Inside a course, you'll find the **Modules** or **Materials** section:

1. Click the **"Modules"** tab

## Assignments and Submissions

Complete and submit assignments online through the portal.

### Viewing Assignments

Access your assignments in multiple ways:

**Method 1: Dashboard Widget**
- See upcoming assignments on your dashboard
- Shows deadlines and status

**Method 2: Assignments Page**
1. Navigate to **Dashboard → Assignments**
2. View all assignments across all subjects

**Method 3: Within a Course**
1. Open a course
2. Click the **"Assignments"** tab

![Assignments List](../images/assignments-list.png)

### Assignment Status Indicators

Assignments are color-coded by status:
- 🔵 **Blue**: Not started
- 🟡 **Yellow**: In progress (draft saved)
- 🟢 **Green**: Submitted
- 🔴 **Red**: Overdue
- ⚪ **Gray**: Graded

### Viewing Assignment Details

Click on an assignment to see:
- **Title and Description**: What you need to do
- **Instructions**: Step-by-step guidance
- **Attached Files**: Reference materials or templates
- **Due Date and Time**: Submission deadline
- **Points Possible**: Maximum grade
- **Submission Type**: Text, file upload, or both
- **Rubric**: Grading criteria (if provided)

![Assignment Details](../images/assignment-details.png)

### Submitting a Text Assignment

For text-based responses:

1. Open the assignment
2. Scroll to the **"Your Submission"** section
3. Type your answer in the text editor
4. Use the formatting tools:
   - Bold, italic, underline
   - Bullet points and numbering
   - Insert links or images
5. Click **"Save Draft"** to save without submitting
6. Click **"Submit Assignment"** when ready

![Text Submission](../images/assignment-text-submit.png)

⚠️ **Warning**: Once you click "Submit Assignment," you cannot edit your submission unless the teacher allows resubmissions.

### Submitting a File Upload Assignment

For assignments requiring file uploads:

1. Open the assignment
2. Scroll to the **"Upload Files"** section
3. Click **"Choose Files"** or drag files into the upload area
4. Select one or more files from your device
5. Wait for the upload to complete (progress bar shows status)
6. Add a comment if needed
7. Click **"Submit Assignment"**

![File Upload](../images/assignment-file-upload.png)

**Supported File Types**:
- Documents: PDF, DOC, DOCX
- Spreadsheets: XLS, XLSX
- Presentations: PPT, PPTX
- Images: JPG, PNG, GIF
- Compressed: ZIP

**File Size Limits**:
- Maximum per file: 25MB
- Maximum total submission: 100MB

💡 **Tip**: Convert large files to PDF to reduce size. Compress images before uploading.

### Saving a Draft

Save your work without submitting:

1. Enter your response or upload files
2. Click **"Save Draft"**
3. You can leave and return later to continue working
4. Drafts are saved automatically every 2 minutes

📝 **Note**: Saving a draft does not count as a submission. You must click "Submit Assignment" before the deadline.

### Viewing Submission Status

After submitting:

1. The assignment status changes to **"Submitted"**
2. You'll see a confirmation message with:
   - Submission date and time
   - Submitted files (if applicable)
   - Teacher feedback (when graded)

![Submission Confirmation](../images/assignment-submitted.png)

### Resubmitting an Assignment

If the teacher allows resubmissions:

1. Open the graded assignment
2. Review the teacher's feedback
3. Click **"Resubmit Assignment"**
4. Make your changes
5. Submit again

⚠️ **Warning**: Not all assignments allow resubmissions. Check with your teacher if you need to revise your work.

### Late Submissions

If you miss a deadline:
- The assignment will be marked as **"Overdue"**
- You may still be able to submit, but with a late penalty
- Some assignments lock after the due date and cannot accept late submissions

💡 **Tip**: Set reminders on your phone for assignment deadlines. Check the portal daily for new assignments.

## Taking Quizzes

Online quizzes are timed assessments administered through the portal.

### Accessing Quizzes

View available quizzes:

1. Navigate to **Dashboard → Quizzes**
2. Or access through a specific course

Quiz status indicators:
- 🔵 **Available**: Ready to take
- 🟡 **In Progress**: Started but not finished
- 🟢 **Completed**: Submitted
- ⚪ **Graded**: Scored
- 🔴 **Missed**: Deadline passed without submission

![Quizzes List](../images/quizzes-list.png)

### Quiz Details

Click on a quiz to view:
- **Title and Description**: Topic and overview
- **Number of Questions**: Total question count
- **Time Limit**: How long you have to complete it
- **Available Date/Time**: When you can start
- **Due Date/Time**: Deadline for completion
- **Attempts Allowed**: Number of tries (usually 1)
- **Question Types**: Multiple choice, true/false, essay, etc.

### Starting a Quiz

Before you begin:

1. Find a quiet place with a stable internet connection
2. Close all other apps and browser tabs
3. Have scratch paper and a calculator ready (if allowed)
4. Read the quiz instructions carefully

To start the quiz:

1. Click on the quiz
2. Read all instructions and requirements
3. Check the time limit
4. Click **"Start Quiz"**

![Start Quiz](../images/quiz-start-screen.png)

⚠️ **Warning**: The timer starts immediately when you click "Start Quiz." Make sure you're ready before clicking.

### Answering Questions

Navigate through quiz questions:

1. **Read each question carefully** before answering
2. **Select your answer** by clicking on it
3. **Click "Next"** to move to the next question
4. **Use the question navigator** (sidebar) to jump to specific questions
5. **Flag questions** you want to review later (flag icon)

![Quiz Interface](../images/quiz-taking-interface.png)

**Question Types**:
- **Multiple Choice**: Select one correct answer
- **Multiple Select**: Choose all correct answers (checkboxes)
- **True/False**: Select true or false
- **Short Answer**: Type a brief response
- **Essay**: Write a detailed answer
- **Matching**: Match items from two columns

💡 **Tip**: Answer easy questions first, then return to difficult ones using the question navigator.

### Monitoring Time

Keep track of time:
- **Timer display**: Shows time remaining (top-right corner)
- **Time warnings**:
  - Yellow warning at 5 minutes remaining
  - Red alert at 1 minute remaining
- **Auto-submit**: Quiz submits automatically when time expires

![Quiz Timer](../images/quiz-timer.png)

### Reviewing Your Answers

Before submitting:

1. Click **"Review Answers"** button
2. See a summary of all questions
3. Identify unanswered questions (marked in red)
4. Click on any question to review and change your answer
5. Check for flagged questions

### Submitting the Quiz

When you're ready to submit:

1. Click **"Submit Quiz"** button
2. Confirm your submission in the popup dialog
3. Review the confirmation message

✅ **Success**: Your quiz is submitted! You'll see:
- Submission time
- Questions answered
- Your score (if immediately available)

![Quiz Submitted](../images/quiz-submitted.png)

📝 **Note**: Some quizzes show scores immediately, while others require teacher grading (especially for essay questions).

### Viewing Quiz Results

After the quiz is graded:

1. Navigate to **Dashboard → Quizzes**
2. Click on the completed quiz
3. View your results:
   - Total score and percentage
   - Correct and incorrect answers (if allowed by teacher)
   - Feedback on specific questions
   - Overall teacher comments

### Quiz Best Practices

🔍 **Quick Tips**:
- Take practice quizzes if available
- Review course materials before the quiz
- Start quizzes early, not at the last minute
- Read all answer choices before selecting
- Don't spend too much time on one question
- Save frequently if the quiz allows it
- Check your internet connection before starting

⚠️ **Common Mistakes to Avoid**:
- Starting the quiz without reading instructions
- Not checking the time limit
- Rushing through questions
- Forgetting to submit (auto-submit saves you!)
- Taking the quiz on unstable internet

## Viewing Grades and GWA

Monitor your academic progress through the Grades section.

### Accessing the Grades Page

1. Navigate to **Dashboard → Grades** in the main menu
2. Or click on your **GWA stat** on the dashboard

### Understanding Your Grades Display

The Grades page shows:

**Overall Summary**:
- Current GWA (Grade-Weighted Average)
- Grade level and section
- Academic period (quarter/semester)
- Class rank (if enabled)

**Grades by Subject**:
Each subject card displays:
- Subject name and code
- Current grade (numerical and letter)
- Graded items count
- Teacher name

![Grades Overview](../images/grades-overview.png)

### Viewing Subject Grade Breakdown

Click on a subject to see detailed breakdown:

1. **Graded Components**:
   - Quizzes (percentage weight)
   - Assignments (percentage weight)
   - Projects (percentage weight)
   - Exams (percentage weight)
   - Class participation (percentage weight)

2. **Individual Item Scores**:
   - Item name
   - Date submitted/taken
   - Points earned / Points possible
   - Percentage score
   - Teacher feedback

![Grade Breakdown](../images/grade-subject-breakdown.png)

### Understanding Your GWA

**GWA (Grade-Weighted Average)** is calculated based on:
- Grades from all subjects
- Number of units per subject
- Grading period weights

**GWA Scale**:
- 95-100: Outstanding
- 90-94: Very Satisfactory
- 85-89: Satisfactory
- 80-84: Fairly Satisfactory
- 75-79: Passing
- Below 75: Failing

### Viewing Grade History

See your grades over time:

1. Click **"Grade History"** tab
2. Select the grading period:
   - Current quarter
   - Previous quarter
   - Full semester
   - Full academic year
3. View trends and improvements

![Grade History](../images/grade-history-chart.png)

### Downloading Grade Reports

Generate printable grade reports:

1. Click **"Generate Report"** button
2. Select:
   - Report type (detailed or summary)
   - Grading period
   - Subjects (all or specific)
3. Click **"Download PDF"** or **"Print"**

💡 **Tip**: Download and save grade reports at the end of each grading period for your records.

### Notifications for Grade Updates

Receive alerts when new grades are posted:
- Push notifications in the portal
- Email notifications (if enabled)
- Dashboard alert badge

### Checking Missing or Pending Grades

View items not yet graded:

1. Click **"Pending"** filter
2. See assignments and quizzes awaiting grading
3. Estimated grading time shown (if provided by teacher)

📝 **Note**: Teachers typically grade submissions within 3-5 school days. Contact your teacher if grades are delayed beyond this period.

### Contesting a Grade

If you believe a grade is incorrect:

1. Review the graded item carefully
2. Check the rubric and teacher feedback
3. Contact your teacher directly through the messaging system
4. Provide specific reasons for your concern
5. Request a grade review

⚠️ **Warning**: Follow school policy for grade contests. There may be deadlines for requesting grade reviews.

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**Problem: Forgot Password**

Solution:
1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on the login page
2. Enter your school email
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click the link and create a new password
5. Log in with new password

**Problem: Account Locked**

Solution:
- Too many failed login attempts locks your account for 15 minutes
- Wait 15 minutes and try again
- Or contact IT support to unlock immediately

**Problem: Can't Remember Email/Username**

Solution:
- Check with your class adviser
- Or contact the registrar's office
- Provide your student ID for verification

### Access Issues

**Problem: Can't Access Certain Features**

Solution:
- Check if you're logged in
- Verify you have permission (some features are role-restricted)
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try a different browser
- Contact IT if issue persists

**Problem: Page Won't Load**

Solution:
1. Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Clear browser cache
5. Disable browser extensions temporarily
6. Contact IT support

### Assignment Submission Issues

**Problem: Can't Upload File**

Solution:
- Check file size (must be under 25MB)
- Verify file type is allowed
- Try compressing the file
- Try a different file format (e.g., PDF instead of DOCX)
- Check your internet connection
- Use a different browser

**Problem: Submission Failed**

Solution:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Verify you're within the deadline
3. Try submitting again
4. Save a draft first, then submit
5. Contact your teacher if issue continues

**Problem: Submitted Wrong File**

Solution:
- If resubmissions are allowed, submit the correct file
- If not, immediately message your teacher
- Explain the situation and attach the correct file via message

### Quiz Issues

**Problem: Quiz Won't Start**

Solution:
- Check if quiz is available (check start date/time)
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Disable browser extensions
- Try a different browser
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled

**Problem: Lost Connection During Quiz**

Solution:
- Don't panic—your answers are usually saved
- Reconnect to the internet
- Return to the quiz page
- Log back in if necessary
- Your progress should be restored
- Contact teacher if answers are lost

**Problem: Quiz Submitted Accidentally**

Solution:
- Contact your teacher immediately
- Explain what happened
- Request a retake if allowed
- Some quizzes allow multiple attempts

### Grade Issues

**Problem: Grade Doesn't Appear**

Solution:
- Teachers need time to grade (3-5 days typically)
- Check the "Pending" section
- If delayed beyond normal time, message your teacher
- Verify you actually submitted the assignment

**Problem: Grade Seems Incorrect**

Solution:
1. Review the rubric and feedback carefully
2. Check for partial credit
3. Verify all parts were submitted
4. If still concerned, politely message your teacher
5. Request clarification on grading

### Technical Issues

**Problem: Slow Performance**

Solution:
- Close unnecessary browser tabs
- Clear browser cache
- Disable browser extensions
- Check your internet speed
- Try during off-peak hours (early morning or late evening)
- Use a wired connection instead of Wi-Fi

**Problem: Videos Won't Play**

Solution:
- Update your browser to latest version
- Enable JavaScript
- Check internet speed (need at least 2-5 Mbps for video)
- Try a different browser
- Download video instead of streaming (if option available)

**Problem: Mobile App Issues**

Solution:
- Update to latest version
- Clear app cache
- Uninstall and reinstall
- Try mobile browser instead
- Check device storage (need at least 500MB free)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Can I access the portal from multiple devices?**

A: Yes! You can log in from any device (computer, tablet, phone). Your data syncs across all devices.

**Q: Is the portal available 24/7?**

A: Yes, the portal is available 24/7 except during scheduled maintenance (usually late at night/early morning). You'll be notified in advance of maintenance windows.

**Q: Can I use the portal during weekends and holidays?**

A: Yes, you can access the portal anytime. However, teachers may not respond to messages during weekends and holidays.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**

A: The portal is optimized for mobile browsers. You can add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

**Q: How much data does the portal use?**

A: A typical session uses 5-10MB. Watching videos uses more. Download materials on Wi-Fi to save mobile data.

### Account Questions

**Q: How do I change my password?**

A: Go to Profile → Settings → Security → Change Password.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**

A: No, your school email is permanent. Contact the registrar if it's incorrect.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**

A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it via email.

**Q: Can I have multiple accounts?**

A: No, each student has one account. Having multiple accounts violates school policy.

### Assignment Questions

**Q: Can I submit assignments late?**

A: It depends on the teacher's policy. Some accept late submissions with penalties; others do not. Check with your teacher.

**Q: Can I edit an assignment after submitting?**

A: Only if the teacher allows resubmissions. Otherwise, submissions are final.

**Q: What file formats can I submit?**

A: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, PPT, PPTX, JPG, PNG, and ZIP files are supported.

**Q: What's the maximum file size for assignments?**

A: 25MB per file, 100MB total per submission.

**Q: How do I know if my assignment was submitted successfully?**

A: You'll see a confirmation message and the assignment status will change to "Submitted."

### Quiz Questions

**Q: Can I pause a quiz and continue later?**

A: Only if the teacher allows it. Most quizzes must be completed in one session.

**Q: What happens if I lose internet during a quiz?**

A: Your answers are usually saved. Reconnect and return to the quiz page to continue.

**Q: Can I retake a quiz if I'm not happy with my score?**

A: Only if the teacher allows multiple attempts. Check the quiz settings.

**Q: When will I see my quiz results?**

A: Immediately for auto-graded questions (multiple choice, true/false). Essay questions require teacher grading.

**Q: Can I review quiz questions after submitting?**

A: Only if the teacher enables quiz review. Some teachers hide questions to prevent sharing.

### Grade Questions

**Q: How often are grades updated?**

A: Teachers typically update grades within 3-5 school days after submission.

**Q: How is my GWA calculated?**

A: GWA is the weighted average of all your subject grades, considering the number of units per subject.

**Q: Can I see my class rank?**

A: Only if the school enables this feature. Check with your adviser.

**Q: What if I disagree with a grade?**

A: Review the feedback and rubric first. If still concerned, contact your teacher respectfully to discuss.

### Communication Questions

**Q: Can I message students from other sections?**

A: Yes, you can message any student in the school (unless messaging is restricted).

**Q: How quickly do teachers respond to messages?**

A: Teachers typically respond within 24-48 hours during school days.

**Q: Can I send files through chat?**

A: Yes, you can attach files up to 25MB per message.

**Q: Are my messages private?**

A: Messages are private between participants. However, school administrators can access messages if needed for safety or policy reasons.

### Club Questions

**Q: How many clubs can I join?**

A: There's usually no limit, but balance your commitments wisely.

**Q: How do I leave a club?**

A: Go to the club dashboard → Settings → Leave Club.

**Q: Do club activities affect my grades?**

A: Club participation may count toward extracurricular records but typically doesn't directly affect academic grades.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browser works best?**

A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions) all work well. Keep your browser updated.

**Q: Why is the portal slow?**

A: Could be your internet connection, browser cache, or peak usage times. Try clearing cache or using during off-peak hours.

**Q: Can I use the portal offline?**

A: Very limited. You can view cached pages and write drafts, but you need internet to submit work or access most features.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**

A: Yes, the school automatically backs up all portal data regularly.

**Q: Is the portal secure?**

A: Yes, the portal uses encryption and secure protocols to protect your data.

### Support Questions

**Q: Who do I contact for technical issues?**

A: Contact IT Support at itsupport@southville8b.edu.ph.

**Q: Who do I contact for academic issues (grades, assignments)?**

A: Contact your subject teacher directly through the messaging system.

**Q: Who do I contact for account issues?**

A: Contact the registrar's office or IT support.

**Q: Is there a user manual?**

A: Yes, you're reading it! Bookmark this guide for future reference.

**Q: Are there video tutorials?**

A: Yes, check the Help Center for video guides on common tasks.
